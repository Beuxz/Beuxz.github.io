📝 About the Project

This project is a full-scale recreation of Pandora from Borderlands 2, built inside Minecraft. The idea started because no complete map existed online — so I decided to make one myself.

The build follows the progression of the Borderlands 2 main story, one area at a time. Every structure is recreated carefully using a mix of hand-building and WorldEdit. To capture the unique Borderlands comic-book style, Sildur’s Shaders are used.

This is a work-in-progress project, developed solo. Some areas can take days or even weeks to finish. The goal is to complete all of Pandora! 🌍

❓ Community Q&A

Q: How accurate is the map compared to Borderlands 2?
A: The map closely follows the in-game layout and story progression. Some compromises are made for Minecraft's blocky style.

Q: How long does it take to build each area?
A: Depending on the size, each area can take a few days to a few weeks since it is built solo.

Q: Do you use mods or tools?
A: WorldEdit is used for terraforming and bulk structures, and hand-building handles fine details. Sildur’s Shaders replicate the cel-shaded look.

Q: Will the map include NPCs, quests, or enemies?
A: Commands for NPCs and mobs can be added to the world, allowing optional interactivity.

Q: Can I use this map for my own projects or servers?
A: Permission is required. Please contact me via GitHub or PlanetMinecraft first.
